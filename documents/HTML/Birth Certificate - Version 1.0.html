1.2. The Requested Extract

Upon searching, it was found that the aforementioned records affirmatively confirmed the presence on the date of the eighth of April of the year one-thousand nine-hundred (900)
and twenty six (6) AD under the number 274 the name Ali with the name of the father being Mohammed Al-Sayyid Al-Najdi
of a Muslim religion, Egyptian nationality, scientist as a job and the name of the mother being Zainab Soliman
Al-Rayyes of a Muslim religion, Egyptian nationality, none as a job dated
the eighth of November in the year one-thousand nine-hundred (900) AD...
The newborn was undocumented on birth, and executing upon a court ruling of the courthouse in Esna to which the Esna Prosecution office 5051
is affiliated, first number April of the year 1926 on 483
